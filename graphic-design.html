Course Overview

The Graphic Design course is an immersive program that blends artistic theory with industry-standard tool mastery. We help you transition from someone who can use a tool to someone who can solve problems visually.

You will develop a deep understanding of layout, color theory, typography, and branding while mastering professional software like Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign.

What You Will Learn

Design Theory

Master color psychology, balance, and the rules of professional layout design.

Vector Art

Learn to create scalable logos and illustrations using professional vector software.

Brand Identity

Develop complete brand identities, from logo concepts to full brand guidelines.
