How It Works

Our 3-Step Elite Intake Process

Simple, transparent, and focused on potential — not just grades. Here is what the journey looks like from application to day one of class.

Step 1: Application Intake

Submit your background, academic history, and career interests through our rapid online form or by visiting our Krishnagiri campus directly. Our counseling team reviews every application personally — no automated rejections.

⏱ Takes 5 minutes
Step 2: Aptitude & Skill Assessment

Clear a short logical reasoning and basic aptitude assessment designed to capture your latent potential — not test your prior knowledge. This is administered online and is designed to be completed in under 40 minutes.

⏱ 40-minute assessment
Step 3: Cohort Onboarding

Get matched with your expert mentor, receive your training kit, join your elite cohort's group, and begin your 2-month transformation. Our team walks you through every detail before you begin.

⏱ 2-month program
