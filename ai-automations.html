AI Automations

Stop doing repetitive work. Learn to build AI-powered workflows that automate tasks, business processes, and customer interactions.

Start Automating Now

Course Overview

AI Automations is specifically designed to help businesses and individuals leverage artificial intelligence to supercharge productivity. This course focuses on using "No-Code" and "Low-Code" tools to connect various platforms with AI.

Instead of writing thousands of lines of code, you'll learn to architect systems that can handle customer queries, generate reports, and manage data autonomously using the power of AI Agents.

What You Will Learn

Workflow Design

Learn to map out and architect complex business workflows and identify automation points.

Build AI Agents

Learn to create autonomous agents that can take actions based on natural language instructions.

API Connectivity

Master the art of connecting different software platforms via their APIs without coding.

Tools Covered

Zapier
Make.com
Typeform with AI
Mailchimp Automation
Slack AI Bots
OpenAI API

Projects You'll Build

#01 Auto-Social Poster

Build a system that automatically researches trending topics, generates content with AI, and posts to LinkedIn/Twitter.

#02 Lead Nurturing Bot

Create an AI agent that monitors your emails, extracts leads into a CRM, and sends personalized AI-written follow-ups.
